What & Why Is Dark Consent?

Dark Consent is a phrase we have coined to describe the pattern of coercion, manipulation and antagonism of modern companies in relation to their users.

The world is becoming ever more polarised and human rights, consumer rights and privacy are under attack from all angles.

Technology was once seen as something created for us but now more and more it is being used against us.
